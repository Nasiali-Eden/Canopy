# Canopy
### One platform. Community action, recycling marketplace, verified transformation.

**Community · Marketplace · Verified Impact**

---

## The Problem

Nairobi's recycling and waste ecosystem is broken at every level — not because people don't care, but because the incentives are wrong and the infrastructure is missing.

- **Effort without proof** — Community cleanups happen, get posted on social media, and the same site refills in two weeks. There is no way to verify that change lasted.
- **Market prices punish the right behaviour** — Carrier bags and soft plastics cause the most harm but earn the least per kilogram. Burning is rational in a broken system.
- **Collectors are the last to be paid** — A collector passes through two to three middlemen before reaching a processor, losing 40–60% of the value of their work at each step.
- **Donors fund events, not outcomes** — Sponsors pay for cleanups. Nobody pays for a place that stays clean.
- **No portable reputation** — A collector with six months of consistent work has no way to prove it. Their history lives in someone else's ledger, or nowhere at all.

---

## The Solution

Canopy is a three-layer platform that aligns incentives across the entire waste and recycling chain — from the community member who picks up a carrier bag, to the processor who buys it, to the artisan who turns it into something of value, to the sponsor who funds the transformation.

Each layer addresses a distinct failure. Together, they close the loop.

---

## Platform Layers

### ① Community Platform ✅ *In Production*

Where impact begins — no blockchain required.

The community layer is a mobile-first platform for residents, youth groups, and environmental organisations to coordinate, report, and build history together.

- **Community feed** — local news, environmental issues, awareness campaigns
- **Event coordination** — cleanup planning, volunteer sign-up, attendance tracking
- **Live map** — dumpsites, water points, recycling drop-off locations, reported hazards
- **Organisation profiles** — residents can join groups, build participation history, earn community reputation
- **Issue reporting** — GPS-tagged photo reports of dumpsites and environmental violations

Community history feeds directly into the marketplace. A member's record of participation is the foundation of their credibility as a collector or creator.

---

### ② Recycling Marketplace 🔨 *In Development*

Where materials move — and value returns to the people who created it.

The marketplace is a direct-to-buyer platform that connects collectors, processors, and artisans — removing the middlemen who currently extract 40–60% of every transaction's value.

**Supply Side — Materials & Processed Goods**

Processors post Buy Orders specifying material type, grade, quantity, and price. Collectors with verified Canopy history fulfill orders directly, paid via M-Pesa, with no broker in between. A collector's transaction history is their leverage — documented proof of reliability commands better prices.

To correct the market's failure on low-value, high-harm materials, carriers bags and soft plastics earn Impact Points on top of cash — making it financially rational to collect what would otherwise be burned.

Materials listed include plastics (PET, HDPE, soft film, carrier bags), metals (copper, aluminium, steel, brass), reclaimed wood, glass, textiles, rubber, paper, and electronics components.

**Creative Side — Artisan & Maker Goods**

Artisans, makers, and designers who transform recycled materials into finished works list on the creative side. Listings are story-driven — buyers see what the object is, who made it, what it was made from, and the process behind it.

Creators who source their materials directly through the Canopy supply chain earn a **Circular Craft Badge** — a verified signal that the purchase closes a complete loop, from collector to maker to buyer.

**How the Two Sides Connect**

- **Material DNA** — every creative listing can link back to its supply-side source, giving buyers full provenance from collection to creation
- **Creator Buy Requests** — artisans post Wanted listings for specific materials; collectors and processors respond directly
- **Shared Impact Scoring** — the same system that rewards collectors for gathering carrier bags rewards artisans who use those materials in their work
- **Fair Value Transparency** — material prices from the supply side inform honest pricing on the creative side

### ③ Verified Transformation 🔨 *In Development*

We pay for outcomes, not events.

Anyone can post a cleanup. Canopy only rewards a place that stays clean.

```
Report        →  GPS + photos + crowdsourced community baseline       (off-chain)
Intervention  →  Validator consensus anchored on Cardano             (partly on-chain)
Transformation →  Sustained change confirmed 30–90 days later         (on-chain)
               →  Sponsor bounty releases automatically
```

Sponsors, NGOs, and government partners fund outcome bounties. Smart contracts hold the funds. Rewards release only when validator consensus confirms that a location has remained clean for the required period — not when the cleanup was held.

## Tokenization & On-Chain Economy

Cardano enters only where strangers need to trust each other. Everything else stays off-chain — fast, free, and accessible.

**What lives on-chain:**
- Collector reputation records and verified transaction history
- Impact Point accrual for low-value, high-harm material collection
- Validator consensus for transformation verification
- Sponsor bounty smart contracts (ADA, released on verified outcomes)
- Impact NFTs — permanent, unfakeable proof of contribution milestones

**What stays off-chain:**
- All marketplace listings, search, and browsing
- Community feed, event coordination, and mapping
- Standard buyer-seller messaging and negotiation

**Payments:**
- M-Pesa is the front-end for all local transactions — familiar, trusted, and instant
- DJED stablecoin is available for larger or cross-border transactions, removing volatility risk for bulk material orders and international buyers

**The long-term vision** is a fully tokenized circular economy where Impact Points, marketplace transactions, and verified transformation rewards all operate within a unified on-chain economy — ADA and DJED as the medium of exchange across the entire platform, redeemable through marketplace purchases, artisan commissions, and partner vendor networks.

## Pilot Location

**Kibera, Nairobi** — one of Africa's largest urban settlements, home to approximately 2,000 active waste collectors who are currently unrecognised, unrecorded, and undercompensated.

Kibera is not a proof-of-concept location. It is the starting point of a local-first scale strategy. The Canopy model launches in one city, builds density, and replicates — preserving local identity at every stage of expansion.
